My Journey

I never expected coding to change my life — until my friend’s older sister, sneaking past her mom and me in the kitchen for some snacks, casually suggested I try learning HTML.

At the time, I was lost in what I wanted to do. I knew I was ambitious and capable of anything I applied myself to, but the question lingered:

“What should I apply myself to?”

Then, with nothing more than determination and excitement, I started to learn how to code.

Once I started exploring front-end development, I immediately felt a spark of passion and knew this was what I wanted to pursue. Since then, I’ve been relentless in learning and growth, holding myself to high standards so every project reflects both my dedication and love for the craft.

That offhand suggestion, offered in a split second before she dashed off with her snacks, ended up changing the projection of my life and journey in coding.

Since then, I have been relentless in my pursuit of learning and constantly hold myself to the highest standards, ensuring every project reflects both my dedication and love for the craft.
